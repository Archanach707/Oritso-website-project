CKYC

Team at ORITSO developed the software considering the CERSAI Guideline.

CKYCR is an Android & IOS based application to ease the load of document scanning, it includes the Maker and checker utilities.

Benefits of CKYC Solution

CKYCR Solution is user friendly for the core audience to achieve the desired objectives.
Our Mobile-CKYC solution enables the branch user to fills and captures image as per the additional details required in the solution.
The application has sufficient data entry / upload module to accept/edit the gap data for a single/multiple customers at a time.

Our Solution converts uploaded documents as per the requirement of CERSAI.

Saves Manpower cost, facilitating faster and efficient access of Documents.

Reduces cost on Physical storage and thus saves Environment.

CKYCR is user friendly with smart Gap Analysis algorithm for easy and faster file generation.

Intelligent duplicate matching algorithms makes Auto Escalation and Dashboard reportig easy.

Easy Maintenence and Customisation as per client requirement makes CKYCR a popular solution.
About the Benefits of CKYC are:

  • MOBILE-CKYCR

    MCKYCR is our Mobile base module to send the KYC Documents to Banks Head Office by any of its Branch anytime.

  • DASHBOARD

    Compliance team gets complete visibility of pending uploads, pending notifications or pending modifications along with ageing analysis.

  • MULTIPLE FORMAT REPORTS

    CKYCR can generates reports in PDF, RTF, HTML, CSV and XSL/XLSX.
