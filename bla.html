Brown Label ATM

Brown label ATM (also known as BLA ATM) refers to an ATM where the machine's money management and connectivity to financial systems.

The systems are provided by a bank (known as sponsor bank) but the machine is owned by the manufacturer (known as a service provider or vendor) and offered under a lease to the bank.

ORITSO Private Limited offers value proposition for deployment of Brown ATM under Revenue share model.

It will enhance Bank customer service by providing 24×7 ATM facilities.

ORITSO provides latest technology & complies with compulsory updates & mandates as advised by NPCI/RBI from time to time.

Benefits of BLA

While operating the BLA, Bank is able to overcome its existing challenges of Deployment,
Maintenace and Technology Updates. Bank enjoy's the below benefits of BLA's.

Customer Convenience cost is lower in the BLA model.

Bank gets its own branding managed at the site by ORITSO.

ORITSO will take care of the Machine repair and maintenance along with its uptime.

Bank will earn decent monthly revenue from performing ATMs which adds to their profits.

ORITSO shall provide latest technology & comply with compulsory updates, mandates advised by NPCI/RBI from time to time.

BANK doesn’t have to invest anything in ORITSO BLA ATM setup. Bank operates on Opex model.
