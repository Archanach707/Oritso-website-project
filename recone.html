RECON

The fundamental activity in bank reconciliation involves matching of individual transactions reported by various stakeholders against each entry in data (CBS).

This process often challenges businesses due to the number of transaction modes, the volume of transactions, varied sources of data, and the inability to consistently maintain the reconciliation on a daily basis. All this creates large bottlenecks and cascading impacts at month-end.

Benefits of CTS Scanners

Our robust matching engine allows to create match rules – as simple or as complex as needed, to
automatically perform the reconciliation as soon as the daily data is available.

Our Solution does not need high technical expertise in implementation.

It includes Aging Analysis, Non-performance analysis, financial/ Non-financial analysis.

The user can define Process Sequencing and Escalation matrix.

User can define user access controls on each modules.

O-Recon gives a seamless Inreface with CBS files for transaction processing.

O-Recon provides solution for more than 15 types of payment modes reconciliation.
